About Us

We are a ‘non-profit’ restaurant and bar near High Street , Tonbridge. We cook elegant, rustic, honest food that flicks your senses from classic, nostalgic flavours to new, inspired dishes cooked by super-skilled young minds. With a buzzing gin-based cocktail bar and a kitchen led by Robbin Holmgreen.
